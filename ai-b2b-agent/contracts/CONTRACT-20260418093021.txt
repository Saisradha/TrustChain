### 5.2 Taxes

(a) The prices stated above are exclusive of GST. The Seller shall charge GST at the applicable rate on the invoice, and the Buyer shall pay the same subject to receipt of a valid tax invoice compliant with GST laws.

(b) The Seller shall ensure timely filing of GST returns to enable the Buyer to claim Input Tax Credit.

(c) Any other taxes, duties, or levies imposed by any governmental authority shall be borne as per applicable law or as mutually agreed.

### 5.3 Payment Terms

(a) **Payment Period:** Payment shall be made by the Buyer within **fifty (50) calendar days** from the date of receipt of the Seller's valid invoice and successful Acceptance of the Goods by the Buyer (**"Net 50"**).

(b) **Invoice Requirements:** The Seller shall submit invoices that are GST-compliant and include the Contract number, purchase order reference (if any), description of Goods, quantity, unit price, total amount, applicable taxes, and bank account details.

(c) **Mode of Payment:** Payment shall be made via RTGS/NEFT to the Seller's designated bank account, details of which shall be provided by the Seller in the invoice.

(d) **Payment Confirmation:** The Buyer shall provide payment confirmation upon request by the Seller.

### 5.4 Disputed Invoices

If the Buyer disputes any portion of an invoice in good faith, the Buyer shall:

(a) Pay the undisputed portion within the stipulated time;

(b) Notify the Seller in writing of the disputed amount with reasons within ten (10) Business Days of receipt of invoice;

(c) Work with the Seller in good faith to resolve the dispute expeditiously.

### 5.5 Late Payment Interest

If the Buyer fails to make payment of any undisputed amount within the Payment Period, the Seller shall be entitled to charge interest at the rate of **12% per annum** (simple interest) on the overdue amount from the due date until the date of actual payment.
