### 3.2 Taxes

(a) The Contract Price stated above is exclusive of applicable GST.

(b) GST shall be charged by the Seller at the prevailing rate as applicable on the date of invoice and shall be payable by the Buyer in addition to the Contract Price.

(c) The Seller shall issue GST-compliant tax invoices as per the applicable provisions of the Central Goods and Services Tax Act, 2017, and relevant State GST Acts.

(d) Each Party shall be responsible for its own income taxes arising from this Agreement.

### 3.3 Payment Terms

(a) **Payment Period:** Payment shall be due within **forty-five (45) days** from the date of the Seller's invoice (**"Net 45"**).

(b) **Invoice Submission:** The Seller shall submit the invoice to the Buyer along with proof of delivery, including delivery challan and goods receipt acknowledgment.

(c) **Mode of Payment:** All payments shall be made by the Buyer through RTGS/NEFT/bank transfer to the Seller's designated bank account, details of which shall be provided in the invoice.

(d) **Currency:** All payments under this Agreement shall be made in Indian Rupees (INR).

### 3.4 Late Payment Interest

If the Buyer fails to make any payment by the due date, the Seller shall be entitled to charge interest on the overdue amount at the rate of **twelve percent (12%) per annum**, calculated on a daily basis from the due date until the date of actual payment, without prejudice to any other rights or remedies available to the Seller under this Agreement or applicable law.

### 3.5 Disputed Invoices

(a) If the Buyer disputes any portion of an invoice in good faith, the Buyer shall notify the Seller in writing within seven (7) Business Days of receipt of the invoice, specifying the disputed amount and the reasons for the dispute.

(b) The undisputed portion of the invoice shall remain payable in accordance with the payment terms specified herein.

(c) The Parties shall endeavor to resolve the dispute within fifteen (15) Business Days of notification. Upon resolution, any amount found due shall be paid within ten (10) Business Days.
